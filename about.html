The journey so far

Hi, I’m John, and here I am with my wife Fay and daughter, Harper. I’m from London originally, but now reside in the leafy suburb of Oakwood in Leeds. My journey to becoming a professional web developer has been quite a long one. I would say I started to learn to code properly in December 2020, but in the previous five years to that I setup and ran the website for my travel business on Wordpress using no code solutions such as page builders and plugins. Over that time I worked with an agency to optimise the SEO, implementing some of the onsite work myself. It also gave me experience of SEO, link building and content marketing and we managed to rank on page one for certain targeted keywords. In 2021 I completed the Northcoders bootcamp and after a short break due to unfortunate circumstances I am now finally looking for my first position as a junior front end developer.
